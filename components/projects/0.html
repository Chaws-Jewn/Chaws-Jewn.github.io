Overview:
Payroll Management System is designed for a driving school which can be categorized under small business. This system involves pay calculation, pay deductions, holidays and monthly contribution configurations, pay slip downloads, payroll downloads, employee management, leave and overtime requests, and more. Pay slip and payroll records data fetching time is greatly reduced due to denormalization of the data once processed in the system (json text in the text field on the database), this helps users in accessing their records immediately and consumes less resources.
Objectives:
As a back end developer, I am responsible for the back end of our system including database structure and functionalities, HTTP requests on the front end, logic and authentication handling in front end.
