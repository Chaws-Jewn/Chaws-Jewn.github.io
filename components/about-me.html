WHO AM I?

I am a BSIT graduate from Gordon College specializing in using back end technologies, hoping to land on a job where I would grow as a developer and contribute to the growth of the company. In my early years in college is all about the fundamentals in programming, starting with the HTML and CSS basics, C, Java, JavaScript, and others. I am a fullstack developer then, controlling all the elements of the application. In the later years, where group work becomes vital, I learned to take over the back end of the application, adhering to RESTful API principles and also programming the logic and service part of the front end: connecting to the back end, authentication, form validation, and more. Though I am more of a back end developer, I still learn designing and interactivity in the front end.
